Welcome to Villa Sofia
Studios Apartments in Parga

Villa Sofia is a small Villa on the fabulous beach of Lichnos. Lichnos is situated in Epirus and is 50 km away from Igoumenitsa and Preveza. The small picturesque coastal town Parga is located about 2 km from here.

Villa Sofia is a house with 3 Apartments. The Apartment on the upper floor is for those who require something special and are interested to be in a place like your house and not just in a small aparment. In the lower part of the house you can rent the other 2 luxuriously constructed Apartments.

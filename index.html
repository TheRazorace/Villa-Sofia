Three separate apartments await you, two on the ground floor and one on the first floor, with capacity of three(3), four(4) and five(5) people each.

The hosts are very willing to recommend you the area’s stunning beaches, the most fascinating excursions, mountain paths, archeological sites, local festivals, Greek gastronomy hangouts and more.

We are looking forward to providing you with exclusive and interesting vacation and we hope that you will have unforgettable experiences during your stay in Villa Sofia and Parga.

Welcome to Parga!


Parga in Epirus is one of the most popular travel destinations! It is an administrative district of the prefecture of Preveza.

The town of Parga is built amphitheatrically with many colorful houses.

From the castle you have a breathtaking view down on Parga and the small island with the church Panagia.

The small harbor promenade invites you to stay, relax or take a walk.

There are many attractions and beaches in Parga and surrounding area which you can visit.

The Venetian castle, ecclesiastical museum, olive oil museum, the small water mill and the Ali Pasha's Castle at Anthousa, the beaches piso Kryoneri, Kryoneri, Valtos, Sarakiniko and much more.

Come and discover the beauty of Parga!

Various excursion suggestions!


In the area of Parga you can find more beautiful Attractions. Many of them you can reach with your own car, rental car or motorcycle. There is also the possibility to enjoy some destinations by bus with a guided group. From Parga or Lefkada you can make also boat trips. Some of these attractions are:

Acheron River: A place of natural beauty, wild rocks, waterfalls and lot of green surrounded

The Necromantion: The mythological Oracle of the Dead. It was believed that there was the access to the underworld of Hades

Nikopolis: The archaeological ancient city near Preveza

Dodoni: The ancient theatre

Ioannina: The capital city of Epirus with its historic center, the lake and many sights

Vikos Gorge: Vikos is listed as the deepest gorge by the Guinness Book of Records

The islands Paxos and Antipaxos
















How to travel to Parga!




Travel by plane

The nearest airports are Preveza/Lefkas (PVK), Corfu (CFU), Ioannina (IOA only domestic flights) and Thessaloniki (SKG).


Travel by ship

The nearest harbor is Igoumenitsa. There are many connections to Italy, for example Venice, Ancona, Bari, etc.


Travel by car

From Thessaloniki via highway Egnatia Odos E90 in the direction Veria, Kozani, Ioannina. Before Igoumenitsa take the exit and continue in direction Parga. From Athens follow the Highway Athens-Patras. Cross the bridge Rio-Antirrio continue to Mesolongi, Agrinio, Preveza and Parga
